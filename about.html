About Me

Jameson Perreault-Perez (Ennea, isthisenne), is a multimedia artist and aspiring game designer who mainly specializes in stylized illustrations with clean shapes and vibrant colors.

From a young age Jameson has always had a spark for bring the stories in their head to life through making games and puzzles out of everyday happenings. Currently their work spans a variety of mediums including digital art, animation, music, sound, and game design. Their upcoming projects include many of these mediums together.

They currently attend Arizona State University where they are currently in the Herberger Institute BFA Animation program, along with a minor in Media Arts with a focus on Games and Interactive Media. They currently create illustrations and character designs of their own original characters, as well as illustrations for games and media they enjoy in their spare time. They have created small soundtracks for small game projects and game jam submissions. They are currently working on their senior thesis film as well as some other ongoing art and game projects.

They also enjoy conlanging, hosting game nights, whistling too much, and subjecting their friends to their game ideas.
