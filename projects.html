Projects

On this page is a list of recent projects (2024-2026) that I've worked on, and my role in them.

Title Project Description My Role Year
The Train That Never Reached Sundown 3-day game jam game created for MiniJam #206. You are a sheriff trying your best to stop a train robbery but you can't quite get to the train in time no matter how hard you try. Music & SFX 2026
Cecil the Centaur 3-day game jam game created for MiniJam #205.Get ready for the climb of a lifetime as you climb to the top of Pony Peak! Music & SFX 2026
Photogratree Game jam game created for ASU's Video Game Development Club's Fall 2025 Game Jam. Fill the shoes of a park ranger helping out a magical talking tree by taking photographs. Explore the woods and find the correct trees to photograph based on the clues you're given and a scrapbook with information about the flora and fauna of the forest. Sound Design & SFX 2025
Rats and Slash Short game created in seven weeks with a small team for a class. Explore the deep reaches of an old sewer system in Rats and Slash, a weapon scavenging adventure hack-and-slash, as a sword-wielding raccoon. Make your way down the depths of the sewers, fighting a variety of treacherous creatures, searching for the Rat King’s throne to claim its treasure for your own. Music, Sound Design, & SFX 2025
Romy the Robot Short game created in seven weeks with a small team for a class. Step into the wheels of an autonomous cleaning robot. Using keyboard controls, guide Romy through different environments, cleaning garbage from a top-down perspective. Collect the garbage in each level in order to pick up the correct appliance without running out of battery. Art Direction, Asset Design 2024
